Introducing Yourself

How to present your background to Frau Hoffmann and anyone you meet on day one — concise, contextually relevant, and confident.

Frau Hoffmann is HR — she processes contracts. She will not ask about your technical background in depth. But she may ask a polite opener like "Was haben Sie zuletzt gemacht?" or "Woher kommen Sie?". Have a clean one-liner ready that is warm and professional without being a lecture.

Core version (for Hoffmann): "I'm a pharmacist by training, did my M.Sc. in Digital Health here in Germany — Deggendorf, finished last year with a 1.6 — and my background is in health informatics and digital systems, which is what the role at the Institut für Medizinische Informatik is about. I'm really looking forward to starting."

That is enough for an HR contact. No need to explain FHIR, DiaTwin, or the Egyptian EHR rollout unless she asks. If she does ask: "Ich habe davor an einem nationalen Gesundheitsinformatik-Projekt in Ägypten gearbeitet — digitale Transformation des Krankenhaussystems. Das ist der direkte Hintergrund für diese Stelle."

How to handle

The §11 BApO permit is a German professional licence issued by the Government of Upper Bavaria — it is German. But it is NOT a full Approbation (independent pharmacy management). On all UKM forms, mark it correctly as Berufserlaubnis §11 BApO, not Approbation.

"Ich habe eine deutsche Berufserlaubnis für pharmazeutische Tätigkeiten — nach §11 BApO, ausgestellt von der Regierung Oberbayern. Das ist keine Vollapprobation, aber die relevante deutsche Qualifikation für meine Tätigkeit als Apotheker."
